About Us

Our Vision

To create a sustainable agricultural sector that strengthens synergy among key players in the production value chain. Our vision is to foster an environment that promotes better agricultural production, ensures food security, and drives economic growth in Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote domestic and foreign agricultural trade and investment by increasing production levels to meet export standards. We aim to modernize smallholder farming systems by providing access to improved agricultural inputs and linking farmers with agronomy specialists and agro-allied companies.

By harnessing the full potential of the agricultural production, processing, and marketing value chain, we seek to enhance productivity, create jobs, and improve the standard of living for Nigerians.

Our Goals

  • Strengthen the agricultural value chain for increased production and sustainability.
  • Enhance modern farming systems through better access to improved inputs.
  • Promote domestic production to drive job creation and food security.
  • Support economic growth and sustainable agricultural development in Nigeria.
  • Ensure the economic and social well-being of our members through continuous engagement and opportunities.
